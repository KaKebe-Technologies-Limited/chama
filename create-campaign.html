Before You Start

ChamaFunds verifies every campaign to protect our community from fraud. To get started, please make sure you have the following ready. Your campaign will be reviewed and activated within 48 working hours.

What You'll Need to Attach

Medical Report or Hospital Letter

Doctor's note, diagnosis letter, or hospital treatment plan — required for medical campaigns.

Supporting Evidence Document

Quotation, invoice, school admission letter, or project plan — for education, business, and community campaigns.

Valid ID / Identity Proof

National ID, passport, or any government-issued identification of the campaign creator.

Active Mobile Money Number

The registered mobile money number where contributions will be sent upon withdrawal.

Campaign Verification & Activation

Once submitted, your campaign will be reviewed by our team within 48 working hours. We verify all documents and evidence before setting your campaign live. This protects contributors and ensures every campaign on ChamaFunds is legitimate. You'll receive an SMS notification when your campaign is approved and active.

Platform Fee

ChamaFunds charges a 7.5% transaction fee on each contribution received. This covers the platform and mobile money network costs, so contributors pay exactly what they choose. Creating a campaign is always free — the fee is only deducted at withdrawal.
