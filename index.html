About ChamaFunds

Pool Money Together for What Matters Most

ChamaFunds is a mobile-money crowdfunding platform built for African communities. Whether you're covering a family medical emergency, funding school fees, building a village borehole, or launching a business — ChamaFunds lets you create a campaign in under 60 seconds, share it on WhatsApp or social media, and start receiving contributions directly to your mobile money wallet. Every donation is recorded on a live public ledger so your whole community stays informed, and funds are paid out the same day — no bank account required, no chasing people down.

FAQ

Questions, Answered.

ChamaFunds is built specifically for African mobile money ecosystems. While global platforms require bank accounts or international cards, we work natively with MTN, Airtel, Orange Money and more — making it frictionless for everyday people to contribute without needing a smartphone app or bank account.
Every contribution is logged on a live public ledger visible to all contributors. Funds are disbursed directly to the campaign creator's verified mobile money number. We also verify campaign creators during sign-up to reduce fraud.
We charge a small platform transaction fee per contribution, which covers our operating costs and the mobile money network fees — meaning contributors pay exactly the amount they choose. Creating a campaign is always free. The exact fee breakdown is shown transparently when you register a campaign.
